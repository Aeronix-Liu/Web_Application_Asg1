Early Life

Logic's early life began as a laid-back astrophysics professor, focused on trivial joys rather than academia.

His romance with classmate Bai Rong introduced him to creating fictional characters —a later key to understanding cosmic logic.

His early academic background in astronomy also laid the groundwork for his later understanding of the Dark Forest Theory—a concept that would ultimately become humanity’s last line of defense against the Trisolaran invasion.

Wallfacer Life

Forced into duty, Logic used his Wallfacer privilege to live reclusively.

He deduced "Cosmic Sociology" from Ye Wenjie's old "cosmic axioms" and dreamed up ideal partner Zhuang Yan.

The PDC found a real Zhuang Yan. They had a daughter, but her hibernation (to push him) made him face his mission.

He uncovered the "Dark Forest Law": expose a civilization's location, and it dies.

Deterrence Era

To test the law, Luo Ji sent a star's coordinates into space, then hibernated. 185 years later, the star's destruction proved him right—he became humanity's savior.

He built the "Dark Forest Deterrence System" (binding Trisolaris/Solar System coordinates) and served 62 years as "Swordholder," controlling the "destruction button." He turned from hero to a feared, cold "deterrence machine."

Later Years

Humanity replaced Luo Ji with Cheng Xin as Swordholder. When Trisolaris attacked, Cheng Xin abandoned deterrence, dooming Earth.

Elderly Luo Ji became "Earth's Tomb Guardian": he worked on curvature ships and guarded Pluto's Earth Museum. When the Solar System turned 2D, he refused escape, staying to die with humanity's relics.
